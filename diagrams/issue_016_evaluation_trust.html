"I found partial information but couldn't fully answer — here's what I know and what's missing"
👍

🏆 Trust = Adoption = ROI
🔑 Architectural Insight
Three layers answer three questions: Are they using it? (Usage) → Is it good? (Quality) → Is it worth it? (Business). You can't measure quality without adoption, and you can't prove value without quality. Build from the bottom up — usage metrics first, business metrics last.
