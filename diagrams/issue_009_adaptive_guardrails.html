🔑 Architectural Insight
Three layers, not one — input filtering catches bad questions, output validation catches bad answers, and contextual gates adapt mid-conversation. Static prompt-based guardrails break when context changes. A rule that works for HR queries blocks legitimate finance queries. This architecture understands context, not just keywords.
